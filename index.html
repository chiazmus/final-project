Welcome to the Guild of Adventurers

The Guild of Adventurers, established in 1340 of the Second Age of Men by Sir Urithrye the Bold, is the place to come if you're looking for Adventure!

The Material Realm is a place filled with monsters, decrepit ruins of bygone ages, and the chance for any noble souls to make untold riches, if only they know where to go!

That's where the Guild of Adventurers can help! Take a look below at some of our highlighted dungeons, or if you're feeling like a team player, you can join some parties looking for another adventurer!
